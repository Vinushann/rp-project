Welcome to ATHENA

Context-Aware Forecasting and Decision Support System for intelligent coffee shop management.

ATHENA is an AI-powered decision support system that helps coffee shop managers make data-driven decisions by analyzing historical sales, weather patterns, holidays, and seasonal trends.

What makes ATHENA different?

Unlike traditional forecasting tools, ATHENA uses a multi-agent AI architecture where specialized agents collaborate to provide comprehensive insights. Each agent brings unique expertise ‚Äî from historical analysis to weather impact assessment.
